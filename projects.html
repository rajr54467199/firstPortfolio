Performance and emission studies of a diesel engine using dual biofuel blend

Diesel engines are widely used in transportation and power generation, but their high emissions of particulate matter and nitrogen oxides can have a negative impact on the environment and human health. One promising solution is to use biofuels, which are renewable and often produce lower emissions than fossil fuels. Our project focuses on the use of dual biofuel blends to further reduce emissions and improve engine performance.
In conclusion, our project demonstrates the potential benefits of using dual biofuel blends to reduce emissions and improve engine performance. We believe that this technology has significant potential for widespread adoption and can help mitigate the negative impacts of diesel engines on the environment and human health.
